The Marauder's Map is a Hogwarts map with magical properties created in the 1970s, before the birth of Harry and his friends, by the four Marauders: Remus Lupin (Moony), Peter Pettigrew (Wormtail), Sirius Black (Padfoot) and James Potter (Prongs). Activated only by saying the phrase I solemnly swear I am up to no good and deactivated by the phrase mischief done, the map is capable of showing the exact location of each person within the boundaries of Hogwarts (their movements are indicated by footprints), as well as indicating paths, secret passages and other points of interest throughout the castle and its grounds.


T.S. Riddle's Diary was a blank diary that Tom Riddle transformed into a Horcrux. This event occurred in 1943, during Riddle's sixth year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, and was the first Horcrux he made. Before Lord Voldemort's first defeat in 1981, the diary was passed to Death Eater Lucius Malfoy, who kept it secret before storing it in Ginny Weasley's cauldron of school supplies in Flourish and Blotts in 1992. The diary used its magical influence to possess Ginny and force her to reopen the Chamber of Secrets. The diary, however, was destroyed by Harry Potter in 1993 with a basilisk fang.
